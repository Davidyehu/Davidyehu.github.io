COVID REGULATIONS

MASKS: Federal law requires wearing a mask while on the conveyance and failure to comply may result in denial of boarding or removal, and subject to fines/being banned from the airline. It’s also the law & rule in every country & airline. Flights to/from the USA require masks for everyone over 2 yrs old by law. In general European airlines are more relaxed with the mask rules for kids till 5-12 yrs old depending on the airline.

USA ENTRY REGULATIONS:

IMPORTANT: Non-US Citizens or permanent residents (green card holders) are banned from entering the USA if they were in the European Schengen Area/United Kingdom/Ireland in the past 14 days. If you are not a US citizen or resident , do not travel to Uman via Europe!!! Even flying TO Ukraine via Europe will ban you from returning to the USA before 14 days. Currently the only way is via Turkey, Emirates, Qatar or our non-stop charter. There is some limited exceptions for children or parents from US citizens. For more info check the CDC’s website.

To Enter the USA, all passengers over the age of 2 need to + a negative Covid test from the past 72 hours (PCR or Rapid), even if you are vaccinated. accompanying the test everyone will need to fill out & sign the CDC attestation Form

UKRAINE ENTRY REGULATIONS:

Non-Ukrainians citizens or residents are required to hold an insurance policy from Ukraine. It’s recommended to have the insurance printed out. You can purchase insurance thru our booking portal here.

– Those who are vaccinated (fully or partially): Do not need a covid test to enter Ukraine or quarantine. Some airlines do ask for a covid test and some transit locations require it so best to have a test done within 72 hours before departure (PCR or Rapid).
– Those who are not vaccinated: Need to provide a negative covid test done within the past 72 hours (PCR or Rapid),
download “Diy Vdoma” app, test again in Ukraine in the next 72 hours, once you have the results upload in the app and you can deactivate the app, otherwise you have to self-isolate for 10 days. Please note if you do not have proof of vaccination or a smartphone you will be denied entry to Ukraine.

If you have “proof of recovery” you don’t have to quarantine/download the app. Only a “proof of recovery” certificate is accepted, antibody tests are not accepted!!

Under 18 years old do not need to register with the app, and under 12 does not need a test.

Diy Vdoma App

– best is to have the app downloaded beforehand ANDROID – IPHONE – APK – and test to verify your number – but do not click “The Border guard did a check-up” as that will start the timer.

– The phone will need to have service in order to be activated, and Ukrainian sim cards can only be purchased after border control, so make sure you have intl roaming.

– we are trying to verify if it works with non-Ukrainian numbers, some reports suggest not, some suggest you need to unblock intl numbers from your carrier. it does not work with Google Voice.

– We have heard of some cell phone rentals offering phones with just this app.

There is a testing site at the airport if you wish to take a test right upon arrival, for more info visit the airports website .

For those traveling with minors it’s suggested to have a notarized letter from the other parent stating that they give permission to travel.

Plan It Rite will be offering our clients covid testing in Uman, which is needed to return to the USA and to deactivate Vdoma app. Please visit our website to prebook the tests. It’s the passenger’s responsibility to + up at the testing site on the specific date & time to have the tests done, no refunds be given if you don’t + up. The results will be send by email, you can visit our office to have it printed out.
